This recipe is what I call a very special breakfast,just look at the ingredients.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving
  • 2 eggs
  • one table spoon of butter
  • one third cup of cream
  • a pinch of salt
  • freshly blck pepper
  • 3 fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs,cream and salt
  2. Melt the butter
  3. pour egg mixture into pan
  4. use in an wooden spatula bring the mixture into center
  5. leave it for 20 seconds
  6. grind pepper over the eggs
You should only make a maximum serving of two.
